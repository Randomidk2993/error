# Privacy Policy

**AniBot** — Operated by Pikaorsmth
**Last updated:** April 2, 2026

---

## 1. Overview

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information AniBot interacts with when you use it, and how it is handled. The short version: **AniBot does not store any personal data.**

---

## 2. Data We Do NOT Collect

AniBot is designed with privacy in mind. We do **not** collect, store, log, or retain any of the following:

- Discord User IDs or usernames
- Server (Guild) IDs or names
- Command history or usage logs
- Message content
- IP addresses
- Any personally identifiable information (PII)

When you run a command, the bot processes your request in real time and discards all related data immediately after responding. Nothing is written to a database or file.

---

## 3. Data Discord May Collect

AniBot runs on the Discord platform. Discord itself may collect and process data in accordance with their own Privacy Policy, which is separate from ours. We encourage you to review Discord's Privacy Policy at:

👉 https://discord.com/privacy

---

## 4. Third-Party Sources

To deliver anime content, AniBot communicates with third-party sources and APIs. These services operate independently and may have their own data practices. AniBot does not share any user information with these services — requests are made server-side and are not tied to any individual user identity.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

AniBot is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly interact with or collect data from users under 13. Since we collect no data at all, no special handling is required, but users must still comply with Discord's minimum age requirements.

---

## 6. Data Security

Because AniBot stores no user data, there is no personal information at risk of being breached, leaked, or misused. Each command interaction is stateless and ephemeral.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any data about you, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns about data handled by Discord or third-party sources, please refer to their respective policies.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be announced in our Discord server and reflected with an updated date at the top of this document. Continued use of AniBot after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
